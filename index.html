About Me

I am a UX Designer and Information Technology Engineering student with a background in Programming. My passion for creating user-centered digital experiences is complemented by strong technical skills.

I specialize in designing intuitive, accessible interfaces that solve real user problems while maintaining technical feasibility. My dual expertise in design and development allows me to bridge the gap between creative vision and technical implementation.

Currently maintaining a 98/100 academic score, I combine design thinking with technical skills, making me effective in transforming user research into functional, delightful experiences.

My approach to UX design is both analytical and empathetic, focusing on creating solutions that are not only visually appealing but also meaningful and valuable to users.
